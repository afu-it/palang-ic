# Palang IC - MyKad Watermark Tool

## üìñ How to Use

### Step 1: Upload Your IC Images
- **Click** the upload area or **drag & drop** your IC images
- Supports front and back IC separately
- Accepts JPG, PNG, and other image formats

### Step 2: Customize Your Watermark
1. **Choose a template** or enter custom text
2. **Adjust position** by dragging the watermark on the preview
3. **Customize appearance**:
   - Text size, rotation, opacity
   - Color and line thickness
   - Background color and opacity
4. **Configure image settings**:
   - White background padding
   - Rounded corners

### Step 3: Download
- **Single IC**: Click individual download buttons
- **Both ICs**: Use "Download Both" (separate files) or "Download ZIP"
- **PDF**: Create a professional PDF document

## üìã FAQ

### Q: Is this tool really free?
**A:** Yes! Completely free, no hidden costs, no premium features, no ads.

### Q: Are my IC images uploaded to a server?
**A:** No! Everything is processed locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

### Q: Can I use this offline?
**A:** Yes! After the initial page load, you can use Palang IC offline. Save the HTML file for offline use.

### Q: What image formats are supported?
**A:** JPG, PNG, and most common image formats supported by browsers.

### Q: Can I customize the watermark position?
**A:** Yes! Click and drag the watermark anywhere on the IC image.

### Q: Does this work on mobile devices?
